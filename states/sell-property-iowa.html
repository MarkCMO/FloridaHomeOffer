Iowa Real Estate Laws Sellers Should Know

Iowa is an attorney state - a licensed attorney must prepare the closing documents. Sellers must provide a Residential Property Seller Disclosure Statement. Transfer tax is $0.80 per $500 of sale price. Foreclosures are judicial, typically taking 5-7 months.

Understanding these legal differences is important when selling property in Iowa. A cash sale simplifies the process because there are no lender requirements, no appraisal contingencies, and the closing timeline is driven by the title search and document preparation - not bank approvals.
