California Real Estate Laws Sellers Should Know

California is a title state with escrow companies handling closings. Sellers must provide a Transfer Disclosure Statement (TDS) and Natural Hazard Disclosure. Transfer tax is $1.10 per $1,000 of sale price, with additional county taxes in some areas. California has extensive tenant protection laws affecting investor properties.

Understanding these legal differences is important when selling property in California. A cash sale simplifies the process because there are no lender requirements, no appraisal contingencies, and the closing timeline is driven by the title search and document preparation - not bank approvals.
