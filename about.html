About FloridaHomeOffer

We help property owners across Florida sell fast, sell fair, and sell on their terms. No agents, no fees, no games.

Our Mission

FloridaHomeOffer exists to give property owners a better option. The traditional real estate process - hiring an agent, staging, showing, waiting, negotiating, hoping the buyer's financing holds - works for some sellers. But for many, it is slow, expensive, stressful, and uncertain.

We built FloridaHomeOffer as an alternative. A straightforward cash purchase with a transparent process, fair pricing based on real market data, and a guaranteed close. We buy every type of property - residential, commercial, vacant land, multi-family, mobile homes, farms, industrial, retail, and mixed-use - in any condition, in any Florida city.

Since launching, we have purchased over 500 properties across Florida, from starter homes in Jacksonville to commercial buildings in Miami, vacant lots in the Panhandle to waterfront condos in the Keys. Our average close time is 12 days, and 97% of our transactions close without any issues.

How We Are Different

Transparent Pricing

We show you exactly how we calculated the offer. Comparable sales, market data, estimated repair costs - everything is on the table. No black box pricing.

All Property Types

Most cash buyers only purchase single-family homes. We buy commercial, land, multi-family, mobile homes, farms, industrial, and more. If it has a deed, we can buy it.

Seller-First Process

You choose the closing date. You decide whether to accept. You set the terms. We work around your timeline and your needs - not the other way around.

Florida Expertise

We know Florida real estate inside and out - homestead exemptions, hurricane damage protocols, title insurance nuances, county-specific closing procedures, and local market dynamics.

Our Values

Fairness. Every offer we make is based on verifiable market data. We share the comparable sales and calculations behind every offer. If it does not work for you, there is zero pressure.

Speed. We know that when you need to sell, time matters. Our team responds to every inquiry within 24 hours and can close in as few as 7 days.

Simplicity. Real estate transactions are complex enough. We strip out every unnecessary step - no staging, no showing, no inspections, no appraisals, no financing delays - so you can focus on what comes next.

Integrity. We do what we say we will do. If we make an offer, we close. Our reputation is built on follow-through, and every transaction is handled through a licensed title company to protect both parties.
